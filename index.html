writer.ly - Project #4 (Capstone Project)

A full stack application that allows users to generate a random writing prompt, as well as add their own prompts to the database, where they can edit/delete their prompts. Time taken to complete: 4 days.

For this project, I was inspired by my love for writing, and hate of writer's block. I wanted to create an application to help fellow writer's get the writing juices flowing, and inspire them to create new stories. Features in progress: separating out fiction and nonfiction prompts, and allowing users to upload completed stories to their account.

Technologies used: React.js, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Express, Node.js, MongoDB, Mongoose.
